Saanich peninsula plant diversity

Over one thousand vascular plant species are known to occur within the Saanich peninsula, represented by 303109 occurrence records. This diverse flora spans two biogeoclimatic units.

Community science contributions

Through the combined efforts of 8500 individuals, the Saanich naturalist community has now documented a total of 1463 vascular plant species. Based on search efforts over the last decade, 70 percent of the historically reported plant diversity has been confirmed and 362 new species reports added to the list. Community members have now documented 53 percent of the plant diversity known to the region on iNaturalist.
